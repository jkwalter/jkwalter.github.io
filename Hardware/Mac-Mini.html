Apple Mac Mini (Late 2006)

About

My family welcomed home this Mac Mini in early 2007, exposing me to Apple computers and Mac OS X. This computer was actually given to me in 2012 as my family upgraded to newer models. Although this computer is quite outdated, I still like to use it for nostalgic purposes. I have also used it for programming; however, I have since formatted the hard drive and reinstalled the operating system, but never reinstalled the development tools. Please feel free to read the Specifications section for more information about this Mac Mini.
